## Meta Ads – E-commerce Fashion (DK)

| Metric | Svagt | OK | Godt | Fremragende |
|--------|-------|-----|------|-------------|
| ROAS (7d_click+1d_view) | < 1,5x | 1,5–2,5x | 2,5–4x | > 4x |
| ROAS (1d_click) | < 1x | 1–1,8x | 1,8–3x | > 3x |
| CPM (DK, Feed) | > 120 kr | 80–120 kr | 50–80 kr | < 50 kr |
| CTR (Feed) | < 0,5% | 0,5–1% | 1–2% | > 2% |
| CPC | > 15 kr | 10–15 kr | 5–10 kr | < 5 kr |
| Frekvens (7 dage) | > 4 | 3–4 | 2–3 | < 2 |

**Note:** Tallene er vejledende for modne konti. Nye konti accepterer ROAS ned til 1,2x i læringsperioden (2–4 uger).
